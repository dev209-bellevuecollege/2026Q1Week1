Large Language Model ‚Äî a neural network trained on massive amounts of text data to predict the next most likely token

The "Stochastic Parrot" Concept

LLMs generate statistically probable responses based on patterns, not true understanding or reasoning

Key Insight

It's incredibly good at pattern matching, but it doesn't "know" anything ‚Äî it predicts
